OUR HERITAGE

A Legacy of Flavor

For generations, the aroma of freshly baked Kahi has awakened families across Iraq. At Gaimer W Kahi, we bring this cherished tradition to Qatar with the same love, authenticity, and passion that has made it a beloved breakfast ritual.

Our Kahi is crafted from layers of delicate, buttery phyllo dough, baked until perfectly golden and crisp, then lovingly drizzled with aromatic honey and date syrup. Paired with our signature Gaimer — a rich, silky clotted cream — every bite is a journey through centuries of culinary artistry.
